Gin & Chronic Chocolate Cake

This cake is as smooth and intoxicating as Snoop Dogg's flow. The gin adds a unique twist, creating a rich, moist chocolate cake that's straight-up chronic.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup gin
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add gin, water, oil, vanilla, and vinegar. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into a greased 9-inch cake pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Let cool before serving.
