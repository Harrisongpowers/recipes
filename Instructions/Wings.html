Drop It Like It's Hot Wings

Inspired by Snoop's hot tracks, these wings are blazing with flavor. They're crispy, spicy, and smoky, perfect for dropping it like it's hot at any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Ranch dressing for dipping

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season chicken wings with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and smoked paprika.
  3. Arrange wings on a baking sheet and bake for 45 minutes, flipping halfway through, until crispy.
  4. Mix hot sauce and melted butter. Toss baked wings in the sauce.
  5. Serve hot with ranch dressing on the side.
