Snoop's Cool Cucumber Cocktail

Chill like Snoop Dogg with this refreshing cocktail. It's crisp, cool, and perfect for sipping on a sunny day, making you feel like you're lounging in the LBC.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces gin
  • 4 ounces tonic water
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • Ice
  • Fresh mint

Directions

  1. In a glass, muddle a few cucumber slices with mint.
  2. Fill the glass with ice, pour in the gin, and top with tonic water.
  3. Stir gently and garnish with cucumber slices and a sprig of mint.
