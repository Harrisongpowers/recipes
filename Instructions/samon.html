Gin & Juice Glazed Salmon

This dish brings the laid-back vibes of Snoop Dogg's classic "Gin & Juice" to your plate. The salmon is glazed in a sweet and tangy mixture, embodying the smooth, chill essence of the West Coast.

Ingredients

Gin & Juice Glazed Salmon

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup gin
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix gin, orange juice, honey, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Brush them with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Generously glaze the salmon with the gin and juice mixture.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until salmon is cooked through and glaze is caramelized.
  6. Serve with a sprinkle of fresh thyme.
